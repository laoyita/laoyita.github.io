Blue Car Rental

We used them for two weeks in June and had no problems. My top tip for Blue Car Rental is not to waste time waiting for the shuttle bus at Keflavik airport. If you turn left as you exit customs towards Hertz/Avis etc and leave the airport building on that side, Blue Car rental is about 300m away just hidden from view by the hotel they are building. The walk is about the same as the distance through the car part to the shuttle bus pick-up and will be much quicker than the wait and ride.
