Install Airalo

1. Go to Apple store to downlaod Airalo app.
2. Inside the App, buy regional eSims Eurolink 3GB 30 Days.
3. Install eSim.
4. Go to iPhone setting->Cellular: turn Airlo line off until arriving destinations.
5. After Arriving, turn Airalo on while turning primary line off or make sure no Data Roaming to avoid the charge from primary carrier.
6. In UK, data service providers: EE(5G),Hutchison(3G), Vodafone(LTE).
7. In Iceland, data providers: Eir(5), H3G and Vodafone (LTE).
