Sunday, 7/2, 2023

The best stop to get off the London Big Bus to visit Kensington Palace is the Kensington Palace stop (34) on the Blue Route. This stop is located just a short walk from the palace entrance. The Blue Route runs from East to West, so you can hop on the bus at any stop along the route and get off at Gloucester Road.
Westminster is a major interchange station where you can change to the Big Bus on either the Red or Blue Route.

Tuesday, 7/4, 2023

Take the District Line to Blackfriars, then walk to ST Paul's Cathedral.
St Paul Cathedral It opens Monday to Saturday at 8:30 (10:00 for Wednesday).
Take the District Line to Westminister (west bound 3 stops) or Tower Hill(east bound 4 stops).
Take the Thames River Boat Cruise (Run by City Cruises) to Greenwich. The boats stop at four different piers: Westminster, London Eye, Tower and Greenwich. The boats leave each pier around every 40 minutes. The first boats of the day leave Westminster and Tower piers at 10am. The first boat from London Eye Pier leaves at 10.10am, while the first from Greenwich leaves at 10.30am. The last boat leaving Greenwich departs at 6.30pm. The last one going all the way from Westminster to Greenwich departs at 16.40. Later services from Westminster only go as far as Tower, with the last service departing Westminster at 7.20pm. The full length of the cruise, heading from Westminster to Greenwich piers, takes 70 minutes one way, 180 minutes return. From Westminster to Tower Pier, it’s 40 minutes one way, 80 minutes return. From Tower to Greenwich, it’s 30 minutes one way, 80 minutes return.
Greenwich It opens everyday.
