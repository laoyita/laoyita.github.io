Big Bus Hop-on Hop-off


Yes, the London Pass includes the Big Bus Night Tour. The London Pass is a prepaid pass that gives you access to over 80 attractions and tours in London, including the Big Bus Night Tour. The Big Bus Night Tour is a 2-hour tour that takes you around London's most famous landmarks, including Buckingham Palace, the Tower of London, and the Houses of Parliament. The tour is narrated by an expert guide and includes commentary in 12 different languages. The London Pass is a great way to save money on attractions and tours in London. It is also a great way to avoid long lines at attractions. If you are planning a trip to London, I highly recommend getting the London Pass. Here are the steps on how to use the London Pass to get the Big Bus Night Tour:
Go to the London Pass website and create an account.
Select the London Pass that you want to purchase.
Once you have purchased your London Pass, you will receive an email with your voucher.
On the day of your tour, show your voucher to the Big Bus Tours ticket office and they will give you your ticket for the Big Bus Night Tour.
Please note that the Big Bus Night Tour is not a hop-on, hop-off tour. You must redeem your ticket for the Big Bus Night Tour within the period of validity of your London Pass.
To redeem the Big Bus ticket using the London Pass, you can follow these steps:
Download the official London Pass app.
Open the app and sign in with your account.
Tap on the "Attractions" tab.
Scroll down and find the Big Bus tour.
Tap on the "Redeem" button.
Show your London Pass to the driver when you board the bus.
Here are some additional information about the Big Bus Night Tour:
The Big Bus Night Tour departs from Stop 11: London Eye (Westminster Bridge Road /bus stop next to Lion Statue, outside London Marriot County Hall).
The tour starts at 18:00 and lasts for 2 hours.
The tour is narrated by an expert guide and includes commentary in 12 different languages.
Tickets for the Big Bus Night Tour can be purchased online or at the Big Bus Tours ticket office.
