Iceland Ring Road Itinerary: Snæfellsnes Peninsula



From Hotel take Ring road 1 -> 54 to Snæfellsnes.
About 50 KM north there is small town Akranes, and a restaurant Gamla Kaupfélagið, Kirkjubraut, 300 Akranes.
also there is grocery store Bonus there.
Stop at
B: Old Akranes LightHouse
C: Ytri Tunga Beach
D: Arnarstapi Cliff Viewpoint
E: Malariff lighthouse
F: Vatnshellir Cave
G: Skarðsvík Beach
H: Snæfellsjökull National Park
I: Grund in Ólafsvík (hote Address:Grundarbraut 8 355, 355 Ólafsvík, Icelandl)
J: National Park Secret Entrance
